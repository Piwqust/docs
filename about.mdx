## why layersense?

layersense is **100% free** and **privacy-first**. your layer data connects directly to your chosen ai provider—no middleman, no logs, no tracking.

### free forever

- **free api tiers** — cerebras, google gemini offer generous free access
- **bring your own keys** — use existing openai or anthropic credits
- **local models** — lm studio runs on your hardware with zero cost

### privacy guaranteed

- direct connection: figma → ai provider → back to figma
- no backend servers or data collection
- local option with lm studio keeps everything on your computer
