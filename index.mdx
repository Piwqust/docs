— what it does

layersense uses AI to automatically rename your figma layers with meaningful, semantic names. stop wasting time manually renaming "frame 45" and "rectangle 12".

**100% free** — connects to free ai providers (cerebras, google gemini) or runs locally with lm studio. no subscriptions, no limits.

— why choose layersense?

- **100% free** — connects to free ai providers (cerebras, google gemini) or runs locally
- **privacy first** — direct connection to your ai provider, no middleman servers
- **smart processing** — handles large selections efficiently with automatic retry logic
- **highly customizable** — multiple naming styles, languages, and custom rules
